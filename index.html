Take It to the Streets

Empowering Chicago's Ward 44 to Win Parking Ticket Appeals

Take It to the Streets is a nonprofit organization dedicated to helping people contest and win appeals on parking violations in the city of Chicago. The group's mission is to educate residents on how to appeal and win parking ticket cases in court, targeting a ward-by-ward basis. The website provides handouts, templates, and resources for residents of Ward 44 to successfully plead their case in front of a judge. The website also includes statistics, links to resources, and a step-by-step guide to appealing parking tickets.

Did you know that 60% of drivers who appeal their parking ticket in Ward 44 win their case?
